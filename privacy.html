Privacy Policy — Becoming

Last updated: [DATE]

Summary

Becoming does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data with the developer or any third party. There are no accounts, no servers, and no analytics.

What data the app stores, and where

Everything you enter — your habits, your daily check-ins, and your written "vision" statement — is stored only on your own device, inside your browser's local storage. It never leaves your device, and the developer has no access to it, no copy of it, and no way to see it.

Data sharing

We do not share any data with anyone, because we do not collect or receive any data in the first place.

Third-party services

The app may load two fonts and a charting library from public content delivery networks (Google Fonts and cdnjs) when you have an internet connection, purely for visual display. These services may log standard, anonymous network request data (such as IP address) as part of normal internet infrastructure, governed by their own privacy policies. The app does not send them any of your personal data, habits, or entries.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect information at all.

Deleting your data

You can erase all app data at any time using the "Reset all progress" option inside the app, or by clearing your browser's site data for this app.
